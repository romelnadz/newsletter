To obtain the desired look, you must consider tile size and design. Different formats can produce various impressions of how we can see a space. Some tiles have the power to expand a room, while others can make it appear shorter. Here are a few considerations you should make while deciding between tile sizes.

A space might feel more extensive and more open with the help of large tiles.

Small tiles make tiny room flow

Choosing smaller tiles can help you achieve a more condensed and intimate design. As less cutting is likely to be necessary, installation will be simpler.
